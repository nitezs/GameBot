# 简介

> 开发商：Micah Boursier

The Multi-Medium is a visually unique 2D puzzle-platformer adventure in which you play as an interdimensional explorer in the multiverse. The world is watching as you embark on a first-of-its-kind expedition to explore parallel universes, but something goes wrong… there is a glitch in the technology and you can’t get back home. Lost in the multiverse, you embark on a journey to find your place in each world and in the cosmos.
Jump, climb, swing, swim, toss, and grapple your way through 10 diverse universes, each with its own distinct art style, setting, and story.
Each level of the game is painted or drawn in a different physical medium, scanned, then brought to life in the game. Absolutely no digital art was used in the making of the game.
Game Features

10 worlds, each with its own distinct art style, setting, and story
New mechanics in each level to keep the gameplay fresh and exciting
7 bizarre boss fights/encounters
Plenty of strange characters to meet
Hidden secrets in every level
