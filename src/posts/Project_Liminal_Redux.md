# 简介

> 开发商：TryHardStudio, Piece of Voxel

Project Liminal Redux is a first-person horror made in realistic 3D graphics. The gameplay consists of collecting notes, solving riddles and escaping from various entities. The main goal is to survive and escape.
You find yourself in a strange place, you are surrounded by many pools, empty shops in the mall and of course loneliness, something is chasing you and you feel it, but turning around … nothing.
