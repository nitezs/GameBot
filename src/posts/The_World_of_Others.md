# 简介

> 开发商：Stefan Kiel

Humanity is experiencing its darkest hour.
Strangers from a distant planet land on earth and want to wipe out the humans. Your wife Laura is being taken by the aliens.
Find B1-12 and face the challenge of finding Laura.
Good luck…
Game Features

Fight your enemies with up to 5 different weapons.
Collect ammunition for the weapons to be ready to fight.
Care for your injuries with medipacks to hold out.
Use your torch to keep an overview in the dark.
But make sure you always carry enough batteries.
