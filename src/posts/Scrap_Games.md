# 简介

> 开发商：QuickBytes Studio, NPC Games

Scrap Games is a 3D side-scrolling beat’em up game with a simple gameplay system that offers many possibilities. Choose your robot character, upgrade it and go through the next stages by defeating your enemies. Prepare yourself and let the fight begin!!! Beat them up!!!
Game Features

You choose your character out of 8 available, at the begining only two are unlocked.
The main task is to fight enemies on your way and surviving through the next stages.
To defeat the enemies you may use fighting skills, all characters have different fighting styles, but you can also use different weapons and many power ups.
You may collect currency and upgrade your character or you can buy new ones.
12 beautiful different stages.
Buy and upgrade characters.
8 characters with different fighting styles.
Many Power ups.
Lots of different weapons to use such as : axes, swords, hammers and guns.
Many various opponents to fight.
