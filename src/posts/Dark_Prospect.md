# 简介

> 开发商：CashcowGames

The gates to hell have been opened and a flood of monsters arises, which are commanded by a powerful being, the overlord
Game Features

FPS: Gather resources, build a base, build weapons and gadgets, survive the attacks and destroy the RTS portals
RTS: Build monsters, assemble squads, cast spells, extend the monsters abilitys, destroy the soldiers base
5 different maps with unique settings
RTS can either be controlled by a player or an AI (Different AI difficulties)
FPS team can be filled with bots
Custom games with a lot of settings for FPS and RTS
Host or join a game via Steam with upto 32 players
Ingame communication: Voice chat / quick chat / chat
