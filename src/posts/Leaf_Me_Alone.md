# 简介

> 开发商：cream

Leaf me alone is a game where a little cleaning robot tidies fallen leaves, empty cans and even toy ducks. This game can bring satisfaction even whilst you are resting. The exciting journey that you’re about to experience with your cute cleaning robot A-12 is now ready to start.
Game Features

Clean up all the grounds by controlling the cute cleaning robot A-12!
Vacuum everything such as leaves, twigs, or even cans!
Please try to carry the big objects when it’s hard to vacuum.
You can clean more efficiently by upgrading your robot.
You can obtain cute and cool loadouts!
Gain true satisfaction by viewing the tidiness of the place where you just cleaned!
There is no competition or pressure when you clean. Enjoy cleaning while you are relaxed and comfortable.
