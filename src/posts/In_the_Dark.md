# 简介

> 开发商：teamkamakiri

“In the Dark” is a roguelike deck-building horror card game. Cooperate with and fight against other survivors, collect cards, skills, items, and souls, and use them to survive in the world shrouded in darkness due to the impact of the meteorite, where monsters and zombies have appeared.
Game Features

You can cooperate with and fight against other survivors.
Whenever you enter safe areas alone, ghosts will appear.
A horde of zombies is a formidable foe.
Defeating a boss monster, you can choose to end the play or continue. You should consider your free time and achievements.
