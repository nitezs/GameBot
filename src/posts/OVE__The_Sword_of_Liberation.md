# 简介

> 开发商：JSJstudio

Elia, a girl of fate sent to free Ella from the tower’s control, must overcome many obstacles with the help of OVE. 
Bring down enemies and gather the Nav’s trace to grow Elia and equip her with accessories and items for adventure.
As the game progresses, the enemies you face will have their own characteristics and various gimmicks.
Attack your enemies by combining different kinds of OVEs and skills scattered throughout the map.
Each ability has its own characteristics.
Players can change Elia’s fighting style by spending the ability points acquired through adventure.
Start your journey to freedom with Elia, the sword of liberation, overcoming the tough battle against the forces of the tower.
