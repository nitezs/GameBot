# 简介

> 开发商：Shoulders of Giants Studios

Get ready for an epic adventure in DownhillMadness, the thrilling first-person game that combines skill-based physics simulation with unique game mechanics. Immerse yourself in a mystical and surrealist world, brought to life with cutting-edge graphics powered by Unreal Engine 5.1. Experience the excitement as you launch hundreds of projectiles downhill, leaving glowing trails and navigating obstacles to score big multipliers. With adjustable shots per second and a variety of unique projectile types each with different physics, the possibilities are endless.
Play Adventure Mode and complete challenges to unlock new levels or compete for the speedrun high score in Arcade Mode across all 32 levels. Survey the action from above with a live overhead satellite view or hover like a bird. Drop forcefields and use the wind blower gun to direct your shots towards the highest multipliers and avoid 0x scoring areas. Enjoy a multi-artist electronic music soundtrack, with a custom song for each level. With an estimated 10-30 hours of gameplay, DownhillMadness is the ultimate single-player experience you don’t want to miss!
